Take a walk through the steps I take when making websites...

one

Question the Client

The 3 W's :
What is the websites purpose? Who is the intended audience? Why a website?
Take the time to understand the client in order to create the optimal product.

two

Confirmation

Confirm that the client and I are on the same page, therefore assuring I am prepared to create a perfect product...

three

Wireframing

Now it's time to hit the sketchbook. Explore different layouts, color schemes, and UI.

four

Decide on the Tech

This is when I decide what editor I will be using. As well as the markup language or CSS preprocessor, and if I will use any kind of framework.

five

Start the Markup!

Now I begin to code up the HTML along with the CSS using JavaScript if needed. Put emphasis on modularity to ease the revision stages that will be needed when the project is complete.

six

We're ready! ...almost

I go over everything I have done up to this point to make sure I can provide reasoning to why I did everything I did. Once I am ready and pleased with my work...

seven

Presentation

Time to show off and explain! If the client doesn't love certain aspects of the finished product, I take note of that in order to go back and make the appropriate changes.
